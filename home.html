About us

Kathford International College of Engineering and Management is a leading college affiliated to Tribhuwan University, which strongly believes in the notion of learning beyond the classroom. Based on that belief, Kathford vows to be a center of knowledge, where students come to learn, sharpen their skills and hone their creative-self, in the journey to becoming an independent thinker.
Standing tall at the heart of Kathmandu Valley since 2003, Kathford is a unique institution, offering an unparalleled educational experience for students. Currently home to more than 1300 students that are enrolled in diverse academic programs in Engineering, Management and IT, Kathford has been consistently producing highly competitive graduates and scholars, forming a strong alumni network.
In hopes of inspiring young minds who wish to have a greater understanding of the world, Kathford offers a unique combination of student- centered pedagogy in conjunction with laboratory, field, project and research works. In addition, Kathford gives you the opportunities to become the best you can by facilitating you with all kinds of resources and engaging you in numerous events and activities, also giving you experience of true personal and professional growth during your time with us.

At Kathford, we offer you much more than just a qualification.
